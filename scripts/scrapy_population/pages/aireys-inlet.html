The suburb posted a 106.78% growth in median prices over a three-year period. This is considerably higher than the gains it recorded over the past year.

On average, rents on listings have reached the N/A mark, reflecting a 0% increase in returns based on the current median price in AIREYS INLET.

AIREYS INLET, 3231 ranks 94th in VIC in terms of median property value increase and capital gains, based on year-to-date figures.

While it ranks 605th on the list of best yielding suburbs for rental properties in VIC, posting a 0% return.
